About me

A blue team mindset shaped by real operations.

From configuring firewalls in Mumbai to building a full SOC environment in Dublin, here's how I got here, and where I'm headed.

My path into cybersecurity started with a friend's referral to an internship at Telenetworks Technologies in Navi Mumbai. I walked in expecting to learn some basic networking. I left with a real understanding of how enterprise infrastructure actually works: subnets, VLANs, firewall rules, and VPN tunnels running across more than 30 banking systems.

Things really clicked when I started analysing telemetry in Splunk, Wazuh, and Nessus. For the first time I wasn't just reading traffic, I was reading behaviour. Patterns. Anomalies. The moment I realised I could think like an attacker to defend better, I knew blue team was where I wanted to be.

I earned my CompTIA Security+ during the internship, then moved to Dublin to study Cybersecurity at National College of Ireland. Right now I'm preparing for Microsoft SC-200, working with Sentinel and Defender almost every day, and looking for Junior SOC roles in Dublin where I can get stuck in from day one.
